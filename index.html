How we make them

Each medallion is one-of-a-kind: your design, precision brass, hand-pressed glass.

01

Your image

Send any image—logo, portrait, artwork. We turn it into a precision CNC brass mold.

02

CNC brass mold

Our brass molds are machined for sharp detail and durability, so every piece is crisp and repeatable.

03

Hand-pressed glass

Molten glass is hand-pressed into the mold. You get the warmth of craft with the clarity of your design.
